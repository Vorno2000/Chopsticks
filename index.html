Chopsticks Game:

Instructions:

Chopsticks is a game where 2 players battle to kill eachothers hands.

Fingers represent the amount of points each hand has and all hands start with 1 point each. On a players turn, they may choose to add one of their hand's points to one of their opponents hands. A hand is killed when it reaches 5 points. Exceeding 5 will cause the points to rollover from 0 again. A player may also use their turn to transfer points from one hand to another.
